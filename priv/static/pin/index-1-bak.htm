Cinnamon Cream Cheese Rolls -Preheat oven to 350 degrees. -Gather desired amount of bread slices, flatten them and cut the crusts off. -Spread a thin layer of cream cheese on one side of each slice. Then roll them up! -Melt a stick of butter (get more as needed) in a glass bowl. Dip each roll up in the butter until covered, but not soaked. Just enough to pick up the sugar. -Spread a mixture of cane sugar, ground cinnamon and powdered sugar onto a large plate. Transfer each roll up to the plate and roll them in the mix. -Arrange rolls on cookie sheet and bake them for 20 minutes at 350 or until slightly crispy! -ENJOY!
